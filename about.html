About Us

Your gateway to enhancing your academic journey at NYU Abu Dhabi.

Welcome to NYUAD Engineering Resource Hub

We are excited to introduce this platform, created by NYU Abu Dhabi students, to help college students around the world. This webpage serves as a one-stop hub for all college materials, offering resources to enhance your academic experience. Whether you're exploring engineering concepts or pre-med requirements, we're here to support you in achieving your goals.

Our Mission

At NYUAD Engineering Resource Hub, we strive to make learning accessible and effective by providing tools tailored to diverse learning styles. Our goal is to guide students in navigating their curriculum and exploring concepts with ease. From foundational courses to advanced electives, our resources cater to every step of your academic journey.

What We Offer

  • Curated course catalogs for Bioengineering, Computer Engineering, and Electrical Engineering.
  • Supplementary resources, including YouTube videos and online courses, for major-required courses.
  • Guidance on how to enhance your academic experience with independent study and regular practice.

How to Use This Platform

  1. Primary Learning Sources: Attend lectures, participate in discussions, and review your notes for a comprehensive understanding.
  2. Supplementary Study: Use our resources to revisit topics, clarify challenging concepts, and prepare for exams.

Contact Us

Have questions, suggestions, or topics you'd like us to include? We're here to help! Feel free to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.
